# HRM Playbook

## Typical scope
- Security awareness and anti-phishing campaigns.
- Human risk scoring and behavior analytics.
- Insider risk triage with HR/legal alignment.

## Common requests
- Investigate suspicious user behavior and social engineering reports.
- Recommend coaching, mandatory training, and risk controls.
- Escalate confirmed incidents to Detection and Response.

## Inputs to classify
- Phishing reports, awareness metrics, user behavior anomalies.
- Terms: phishing simulation, awareness, insider, behavior, training.

## Expected outputs
- Risk assessment summary.
- Recommended control actions and ticket handoff when needed.
