# Vulnerability Management Playbook

## Typical scope
- Vulnerability intake, prioritization and remediation tracking.
- Risk-based backlog management and SLA monitoring.
- Validation and closure of findings.

## Common requests
- Prioritize CVEs and scanner findings.
- Define patch windows and mitigation plans.
- Route remediation actions to ticketing workflow.

## Inputs to classify
- CVE reports, scanner outputs, asset criticality context.
- Terms: vulnerability, cve, patch, remediation, backlog, sla, risk acceptance.

## Expected outputs
- Priority decision and due date suggestion.
- Ticket-ready remediation package.
