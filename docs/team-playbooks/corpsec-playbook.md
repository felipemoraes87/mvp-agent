# CorpSec Playbook

## Typical scope
- Corporate security controls, physical and office-related security interfaces.
- Third-party and enterprise policy adherence.
- Device and endpoint governance coordination.

## Common requests
- Investigate policy violations and corporate security exceptions.
- Assess vendor and internal policy compliance requests.
- Create action plans and track via tickets.

## Inputs to classify
- Corporate policy incidents and control exceptions.
- Terms: policy violation, corporate security, office, vendor risk, compliance.

## Expected outputs
- Policy impact summary.
- Corrective action recommendation.
